Professional Profile

Dr. Sofiia Alexandrovna Makedonskaia is a highly qualified physician with over 15 years of experience in oncology, gynecology, endoscopy, and psychiatry. Throughout her career, she has performed more than 10,000 complex surgeries, saving the lives of thousands of women worldwide, including in Russia and the USA.

Dr. Makedonskaia is a leader in her field, implementing innovative treatments for cervical pre-cancerous conditions, showing 100% effectiveness in eliminating HPV. She actively participates in international conferences and professional development programs, bringing high technologies to Russian hospitals. Besides clinical practice, Dr. Makedonskaia engages in scientific work, organizes webinars and masterclasses for colleagues, contributing to the development of medicine on an international level.
